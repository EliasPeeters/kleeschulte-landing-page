Cold Pressed and Native

Gently cold-pressed from the core – Moritz premium edible oils.

Our oils Moritz Rapeseed Oil Classic and Bio as well as Moritz Sunflower Seed Oil Bio are produced in a gentle cold pressing process without additives, completely naturally and bottled in a controlled manner.

In contrast to oils produced on a large scale, the valuable ingredients and a juicy, nutty aroma are retained. The rapeseed or sunflower seeds are transported to the oil mill via a 3-stage cleaning process. The golden-yellow rapeseed oil or the light-yellow sunflower seed oil is extracted from the kernels in a gentle cold-pressing process. The press must not press the oil out of the cores too quickly.

The filters are precisely matched to achieve the desired purity of Moritz premium edible oils. Additives have no place in our kernel oil. Finally, independent institutes check the quality. Only then can the cold-pressed premium cooking oil be filled into the bottles and be called Moritz from now on.

COLD PRESSED = HIGH CONTENT OF NATURAL PROTECTIVE SUBSTANCES

Mother Nature has equipped the rapeseed with a number of protective factors, which also protect the cold-pressed rapeseed oil made from it from oxidative processes and thus from spoilage.

Canola oil is usually a good choice. It contains a lot of the essential unsaturated omega-3 fatty acid "alpha-linolenic acid" as well as vitamin E. Rapeseed oil has the lowest proportion of saturated fatty acids and a high proportion of monounsaturated fatty acids. Of course, the favorable ratio of omega-3 to omega-6 fatty acids in rapeseed oil is also worth mentioning.

The oil obtained from the bright yellow flowering rapeseed is recommended by more and more nutritionists as a very good and valuable oil for everyday cooking.

The cold-pressed rapeseed oil is characterized by its balanced fatty acid composition and a fine, nutty and particularly mild taste. After pressing, rapeseed oil is simply filtered and rightly deserves the attribute "native" (= "natural").

Tips for storing premium edible oils

Vegetable oils should be consumed daily because of their valuable fatty acids, but only in small amounts because of their high energy content. Rapeseed oil is particularly recommended because it contains a high proportion of omega-3 fatty acids.

Proper storage is important as oils are prone to oxidation. Depending on the type of oil, they can then quickly become rancid.

Unlike some other varieties, rapeseed oil can be stored for up to twelve months.

Always store Moritz Premium edible oils in a dark and cool place at 6 to 10 degrees Celsius.
